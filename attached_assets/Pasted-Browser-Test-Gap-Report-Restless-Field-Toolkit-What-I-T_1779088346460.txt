The Core Problem
The entire strategy document is built around migrating from sessionStorage to IndexedDB. Right now, every opportunity, draft, and form entry disappears when the user closes the tab. For a field sales tool used on mobile by reps between calls, that's a data-reliability showstopper.

Everything else in the document (BroadcastChannel, pure reducers, PWA routing) flows from that single foundational change.
